Topic outline


    • Welcome to the TESSA Pan-Africa English main menu.

      TESSA materials have been created and developed by experts working across a range of African countries. They are provided under an open creative commons licence and can be used and adapted for your own context as required.

    • Curriculum framework

      An outline of all the TESSA modules can be found in the following curriculum overviews:

    • Primary Curriculum framework Unit content
    • Secondary Science Curriculum framework Unit content

    • Subject resources

    • The subject resources are divided into six subject areas which cover the primary school curriculum and selected topics from lower secondary science. Each subject area contains three modules which in turn contain five study sections to support school-based education and training of teachers. These resources are useful for teachers and teacher educators.
